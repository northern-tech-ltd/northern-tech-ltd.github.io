About

Northern Tech LTD is a private limited company registered in England and Wales. We design and develop native mobile software for the iOS and Android platforms.

What we build

Our focus is software for people whose work happens away from a desk — contractors, field workers, and other independent professionals. The common thread across our products is reducing the administrative overhead of running a small one-person business: tax records, compliance certificates, invoicing, and similar.

How we work

We're a small UK-based team. We ship software directly to end users via the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, and operate a subscription model where appropriate. We use UK-based payment processors and EU-hosted data infrastructure.

Compliance

Our products are built with the UK regulatory environment in mind from the outset: HMRC for tax-related features, Companies House for company records, and FCA-authorised processors for payment and (where applicable) Open Banking integrations. We are committed to UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018 across all of our products.
