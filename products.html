Products

Northern Tech LTD publishes mobile applications focused on cutting the administrative overhead of self-employed and small-business work in the UK.

In development

Our first product is a mobile admin tool designed for self-employed workers in the UK offshore energy sector. It helps users track safety-critical certificates, photograph and categorise expense receipts, raise invoices with a tap-to-pay link, and view a live estimate of their UK Self Assessment tax liability.

Release is planned for the App Store and Google Play in 2026.

Future products

We have additional mobile products planned for other groups of UK independent workers. Each will share the same focus on reducing admin friction and on UK regulatory fit.
